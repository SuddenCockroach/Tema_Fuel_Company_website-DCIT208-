Why Us

Our terminal boasts an impressive storage capacity of 80,000 MT for Diesel (AGO)

Premium Motor Spirits (PMS), along with 4,000 MT capacity tanks dedicated to storing Liquified Petroleum Gas (LPG).

We provide gantry loading facilities for the aforementioned fuels.

Our operations are efficiently supported by the CBM pipeline, enabling us to cater to all licensed Bulk Distribution Companies in Ghana.

With a focus on safety and security, our highly automated Terminal ensures the reliable receipt, storage, and distribution of our customers' products.

We offer a trusted environment for handling your fuel requirements with utmost care.
